Introduction

Meet Malcolm Bowers

Meet our protagonist, a 28-year-old Army Non-Commissioned Officer (NCO) who has spent the better part of their life dedicated to serving their country. Now, as they stand at the crossroads of their career, they are embarking on an exciting new mission: transitioning to become a civilian software developer.

This is not just a career change; it's a transformational journey filled with challenges, opportunities, and the unyielding determination that characterizes a dedicated soldier. Our protagonist's story is a testament to the incredible resilience, adaptability, and skills cultivated in the military, and how these qualities are poised to thrive in the dynamic world of software development.
